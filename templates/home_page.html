Who are we here for?

Here at Fields Meet Finds we are dedicated to providing intuitive and accessible pathways to your data. We serve both farmers' and archaeologists' needs by providing customisable queries. Currently, we offer bivariate and interactive visualisation and we anticipate integrating more variables into the future. In addition to spatial visualisation of your data, we also offer customisable querying to allow you to extract exactly the data you seek.

Farmers

As a farmer, it is important for you to know exactly which of your fields are where, how big they are, which crop the field(s) contain and the relevant growth season. Through our customisable query interface, you can find out exactly what you need.

Archaeologists

As an archaeologist, it is important for you to know exactly which of your finds are where, which era they're from, the material they are made of, what they were used for and any relevant field notes. Through our customisable query interface, you can find out exactly what you need.
